Short bio

I am an F.R.S.-FNRS Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Transportation and Urban Mobility at the School of Engineering of the University of Liège. A native of Casablanca, Morocco, I hold a PhD in Applied Sciences (2017) from the University of Liège, Belgium. I received a M.S in Mechanical Engineering (2012) from the Institut Gramme and a M.S. in Management Sciences (2015) from HEC Liège, both in Belgium. I have previously been a Visiting Research Fellow at Ecole Polytechnique de Montréal, Canada. I have been awarded a PBEEE excellence scholarship (2017) as well as a Wallonia Brussels International (WBI) World Excellence Fellowship (2020). In May 2021, I got the Professor Piet Rietweld Award for the best doctoral dissertation in transportation research from BIVEC/GIBET. My work appeared in major transportation-related academic journals, including Transportation Research Part B: Methodological, Transportation Research Part C: Emerging Technologies, Journal of Transport Geography, Transport Policy, Expert Systems with Applications, etc.
