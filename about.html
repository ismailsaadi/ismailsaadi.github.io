Short bio

I'm examining how people adapt travel behaviours to new urban models (15-minute city, LTNs, car-free zones, superblocks, cycling infrastructure, active travel) and how interactions with the physical environment shape exposures (air quality, noise, physical activity, green/blue space access, traffic injuries, heat). These collectively impact population health, including morbidity and mortality. I was actively involved in JIBE and GLASST. Now, I contribute to DARe.
